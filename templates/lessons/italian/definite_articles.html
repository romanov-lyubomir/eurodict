Definite articles and their usage

The italian definite articles are only 6 (il, lo, la, l', i, gli, le) and they are very easy to use
Article Usage
il il is used most singular masculine nouns
lo lo is used for exceptional masculine nouns*
la la is used for feminine nouns
l' l' is used for singular nouns that start with a vowel
i i is used for regular masculine plural nouns
gli gli is used for masculine nouns that use lo or l'
le le is used for all feminine plural nouns

Exceptional nouns are those that start with s+consonant/z/x.

Article Example
il il ragazzo, il bicchiere, il vino
lo lo zaino, lo studente, lo xilofono
la la ragazza, la tazza
l' l'amico
i i ragazzi, i colori, i limoni
gli gli zaini, gli amici
le le ragazze, le donne
